NIDUM

interactive installation

cane, hemp rope, soil, clay, plants, crystals, pen, paper, fairylights

2022.

(latin = nest) is a transformation place where the visitor could make a talisman out of crystals, shells and clay for themselves, while inviting them for internal reflection on something thet wanted to manifest. The installation gave the recipients space to connect with themselves and each other as well while creating. After traversing the three stations:

1. selecting the raw material,

2. formulating what would gives them joy or what they would like to release,

3. creating the talisman, they could choose to leave their creations in the installation space or take their works with themselves.

I believe the power of ‘manifestation’ lies in the conscious intention one creates to bring something into their lifes. Through reflecting on what one desires, appreciates the experience and attitude changes, helping cultivate gratitude.

The talismans that had been left in the NIDUM were placed in public spaces spreading the joy they embodied. I asked the last participants to help me find the talismans' temporary homes and send me pictures when they succeeded.

The installation is a result of the collaboration between Franci Kas x AZVLM x BOLYGÓ.
