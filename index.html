FRANCI KAS I am Franci Kas. a curious human being who uses the creative process to question, search, find and self-reflect. In my artistic practice I love to take time to explore my subject in depth, journaling, drawing, taking photos and reading are the tools I use most often. I am deeply intrigued by psychology, sociology, neuropsychology, politics and philosophy. I am interested in how art and science can collaborate. Currently, I focus on working with interactive, installative mediums through which I intend to create a connection between my work and its recipient(s) by inviting them to participate, connect and reflect. As for mediums and materials, I enjoy working with new media, sound, textile and I also like curating small books of drawings and photographs.
