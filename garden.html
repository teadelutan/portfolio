HEALING GARDEN

performance

created with Marlene Aigner, Adrienn Fejős, Charlene Galea and Johanna Szőke

2021.

A performance exploring vulnerability, anxiety, connectedness and sisterhood. We connected for 5 days and got to know each other while feeling the pressure to produce a work of art at the end of the week. anxiety came up then came resilience, through holding space for each other and co-regulating our emotions concerning our duty as an artist (to be individual, intelligent, productive, open enough etc) we realised how the whole process of creating a safe space between us and building trust actually is a work of art. Our final performance reflected on this experience, aiming to lean in into our vurnelability, together.

A zine about the whole porocess is in the making.

photos by Boglárka Zellei and Vince Támba

The performance was created within the framework of School of disobedience’s Healing Garden program.
