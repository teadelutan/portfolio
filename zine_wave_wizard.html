WAVEs WIZARD

zine

cardboard, linen, paper, thread, colored pencil

2022.

A highly personal collection of pencil drawings inspired by my experience with a wave wizard. The images echo the feelings of desire, longing, connectedness, distance and vulnerability. The pages are in chronological order starting from 25th June 2022. The last few pages are still empty as the story is still unfolding.
