FEMME RAGE

sound

2022-2023-

Femme rage is a collection of sounds made from recordings of my voice. I manipulated my silent and loud screams and shouting digitally, mixed them creating a tense and mysterious atmosphere for repressed emotions to find catharsis. I performed my electroacoustic improvisational piece in live, accompanied with Charlene Galea’s improvised movements.
