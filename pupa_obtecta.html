PUPA OBTECTA

interactive installation

textile, soil, headset, pen, paper, fairylights

2021.

A human sized archetypal pupa, a sacred space of metamrphosis. Entering the pupa, while listening to the sound I created for the experience, one could write down something they wanted to let go, then bury it in the soil. I facilitated the whole process, telling the participants the steps to take, giving them a paper and a pen, the headset, waiting for them outside. I did not read any of what they had written as it was my conscious decision to keep their intimate thoughts in the soil.
